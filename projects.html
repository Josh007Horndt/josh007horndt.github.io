Project: Recruit (SQL)

This project analyzes over 700 Division-I recruiting records (2015–2020) using a multi-table SQL pipeline to identify the high schools that consistently produce elite college-football talent. By aggregating rankings, positions, and NFL draft outcomes across six recruiting classes, the analysis highlights the strongest national pipelines—revealing which programs most reliably generate top-150 prospects, five-star recruits, and future NFL players. The project demonstrates advanced SQL querying, data modeling, and applied sports-analytics methodology.

Example Database

This is a preview of a database I designed to create SQL scripts to analyze and map the best high schools that produce the best athletes in the nation for college football. In the main database, I have over 700 rows of data including the top 100 high school football players from the past 10 years. Due to confidentiality, I decided to only share a fraction of this data.
